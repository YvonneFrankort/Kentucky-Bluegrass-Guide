Quick Facts About Kentucky

Horse Capital

Lexington is known as the Horse Capital of the World and a center of American horse breeding.

Bourbon Trail

95% of the world’s bourbon is produced in Kentucky with over 100 distilleries.

Bluegrass Music

The state gave its name to the Bluegrass music genre and remains a heart of country sound.

Natural Beauty

Home to Mammoth Cave, Red River Gorge and the rolling green hills of the Bluegrass region.
